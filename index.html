English Teacher

Do you feel nervous speaking English? Does your English stop you doing the things you'd like to do? Or make your job or studies more difficult?

Speaking a foreign language is very hard, so don't feel bad if you need help with this. In my private English lessons for adults, I'll help you improve your confidence, fluency, pronunciation and vocabulary so that soon you'll love speaking and learning English!

In your English class, I can also help you with your reading, writing and listening so that you feel happy using English in all areas of your life.

What will my English class be like?

During your English class, I can provide topics to talk about or we can simply chat about things you enjoy. This will improve your confidence and fluency. We can also do exercises on pronunciation and learn new vocabulary.

I offer personalised English classes in Sheffield or online that are specially designed for you. We can work on anything you find challenging or hard.

Most of all, I hope you will find my English lessons really enjoyable and fun!

Inclusive English classes

If you are neurodivergent and you feel comfortable telling me, please do so. I can try to make sure the environment (noise, lighting) and the way we work together is best for you. See inclusivity page for more details.
