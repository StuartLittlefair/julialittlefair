About Me

I am a TEFL qualified English tutor and ex-translator.

I've worked with languages for a long time. First I was a translator, and then I became a private English teacher. I've been teaching English in Sheffield for many years and I’ve helped lots of different people learn English (from university students to retirees).

I speak Catalan and Spanish myself so I understand how difficult it is to speak a foreign language. My main goal is to help you feel relaxed and confident when you speak English.
